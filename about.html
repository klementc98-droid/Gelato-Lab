Our Story

A calm lab for handcrafted gelato

Since 2012, Gelato Lab has been quietly refining gelato in Karlskrona. We blend the precision of a small-batch kitchen with the simplicity of Scandinavian design to create a tasting experience that feels honest, slow and memorable.

Today you will find seasonal gelato, specialty coffee, Italian hot chocolate and Belgian waffles—crafted with ingredients you can recognize and a process you can trust.

Our craft

Small batches, refined process

We produce every gelato batch in-house and serve it the same day. That means clean texture, vibrant flavor and no industrial shortcuts.

Our ingredients

Pure, natural, transparent

Only real cream, milk, sugar, fruit, nuts and premium chocolate. No stabilizers, no artificial flavors, no hidden ingredients.

Our menu

Seasonal and intentional

Our flavor lineup is intentionally small and deliberately changing. Classics stay, while seasonal experiments rotate with the produce and the mood.

Our space

Quiet, calm, welcoming

Soft olive tones, natural materials and simple lighting create a studio where tasting feels comfortable and unhurried.

How we work

A practical approach to premium gelato

Gelato Lab is built around the belief that quality comes from care, not complexity. We keep our menu focused so we can make every batch with precision, control texture through temperature, and develop every flavor until it feels balanced.

We are a working studio first: ingredients are tested, paired and refined daily. When you visit, you're tasting the result of that process.

Texture first

We chase clean, smooth gelato—soft enough to enjoy immediately, dense enough to carry flavor.

Seasonal focus

Our rotation is anchored by classics and refreshed each week with fruit, spice and local inspiration.

Thoughtful pairing

Gelato pairs with coffee, waffles and chocolate drinks. We create options that work together, not separately.

Our menu is small by design. We prefer to serve what we can make exceptionally well, rather than everything.
