Frozen experiments — fourteen years in.

A small gelato lab in Karlskrona — since 2012.

Every batch is churned slowly in small quantities — fruit, dairy and chocolate chosen for character, never shortcuts. A small Scandinavian studio in Karlskrona, refining the same craft each day since 2012.
